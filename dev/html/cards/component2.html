Cards

Cards serve as a very flexible and intuitive UI element. They keep with our design principles in the following ways:

  • They are familiar to the user, as they are common in some of the most popular user interfaces.
  • Provide immediate feedback, as cards can react to user input with animations, and "loading" states for asyncronous content.
  • They increase user focus by clearly separating important content and controls.
  • They provide great structure for organizing content and UIs.
  • They are simple to implement into our designs, and also simple for the user to intuitively understand.

This is a card

Cards can hold app elements like forms, documents, data, controls, etc. They can have different z-levels, and have appear/disappear transition animations.
