Type & Controls

Copy

As mentioned in the Fonts section, according to Encore's guidelines, we will use Helvetica Neue as the site's font, with Arial as a secondary "fallback" font whenever Helvetica is not present in the user's device.

The font and style guidelines for copy text is outlined in this page. To keep with our principle of Consistent and Familiar Interface, we should adhere to these guidelines in all of our site's pages.



To see font styles for the site's headings, please see Headings.
