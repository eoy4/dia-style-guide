Type & Controls

Inputs & Buttons

The controls, inputs, and buttons of our UIs should adhere to our principles, taking into account the following:

  • Consistency
    • Maintain consistent format across different tools and forms throughout our site.
    • Buttons and inputs shuld have a consistent look and feel everywhere in our site.
  • Visibility and User Focus
    • Using the brand color palette, use different colors for buttons to indicate importance and intent.
  • Instant Feedback
    • Buttons have a subtle hover animation
    • If field sets or controls need to appear/disappear, they should do so with a simple animation to guide the user's attention.
  • Structure
    • Observe the Structure principle when designing forms and controls, keeping elements together or sepparate according to function and flow.
  • Simplicity
    • Do not over-complicate forms or controls. Keep the functionality easy to use and simple to understand.
